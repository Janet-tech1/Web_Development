Home Page

Welcome to Olabams Farms, your premier destination for fresh poultry and rabbit products. Our farm is dedicated to providing high-quality meat and eggs to our customers while maintaining a committment to animal welfare and sustainability.

About Us

  • Our Story: Olabams Farms was founded in 2010 by a passionate farmer who wanted to bring fresh, healthy, and sustainable food to the community. over the years, we've grown into a thriving farm that supplies poultry and rabbit products to customers across the regio. We're proud of our heritage and committed to continuing our tradition of quality and excellence

Meet the Team

  • Mrs Grace, Farm Manager: I've been working on the farms for over 24 years and I'm proud to be part of the Olabams farms team. We prioritize animal welfare and sustainability in everything we do.
